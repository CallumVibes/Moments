Moment's

Your photos. Your people. Your network.

Moments isn't another social media app. It's a radically simple way to create your own private space for sharing photos with the people who actually matter — no accounts, no algorithms, no endless feeds.

How it works

Create a network in seconds. Invite friends by showing them a QR code or sharing a simple join code. That's it. Everyone in the network can see and share photos with each other. Nobody else can.

No friend requests. No public profiles. No data mining. Just a private photo space for your friend group, your family, your team, your event, or your community.

No accounts. No friction.

We built Moments on Nostr, a decentralized protocol that lets you share content without needing to sign up for yet another service. You don't need an email. You don't need a password. You just need people you want to share memories with.

The technology fades into the background. The focus stays where it belongs: on the photos and the people in them.

Why Moments?

Social networks today are built for scale. Moments is built for closeness.

No company in the middle. Moments doesn't live on some corporation's servers. There's no headquarters, no database of your life — nothing to be sold, leaked, or shut down.
No ads. No trackers. Nothing watches what you look at, and nothing follows you around the internet. Your attention isn't the product here — there is no product.
No algorithm. No influencers. Nothing decides what you see, and there's no fame to chase. Just your people, in order, as it happened.
Your data can't be mined — we never have it. Photos are encrypted on your phone before they go anywhere. Only your network holds the key.
Effortless to join. Show a QR code. Share a few digits. Your friends are in.
Yours to run. You control who belongs. The network lives as long as your group wants it to. This is social media you own.

Who is it for?

A weekend trip with friends. A creative project with collaborators. A wedding. A club. A team. Any group of people who want to share photos without giving them to a corporation.

Create your network. Share the code. Capture the moment.
